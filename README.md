# Customer-Shopping-Behaviour-Analysis

## Objective

The objective of this project is to perform exploratory data analysis on customer shopping data to uncover meaningful insights about customer behavior. The analysis focuses on identifying trends, purchase patterns, and key factors influencing buying decisions, which can support business strategies and improve customer targeting.

## Tools Used

* Python (Pandas, Matplotlib)
* MYSQL
* Power BI

## Key Insights

* The average purchase amount is approximately $59.76, indicating a moderate spending pattern among customers.

* Footwear and Clothing categories generate the highest average revenue, showing strong customer interest in these product segments.

* Female customers have slightly higher average spending compared to male customers, suggesting marginally higher purchasing power.

* Most customers prefer digital payment methods, with PayPal being the most commonly used payment option.

* Customers most frequently make purchases every 3 months, followed by annual and quarterly shopping patterns.

* A significant number of transactions occur without discounts, and surprisingly, average purchase value remains slightly higher when no discount is applied.

* The customer age ranges from 18 to 70, with an average age of around 44 years, indicating a diverse customer base.

* Subscription status and previous purchase history indicate that repeat customers contribute consistently to overall sales.

##  Conclusion

This project successfully analyzed customer shopping behavior to uncover meaningful patterns in purchasing habits. The analysis revealed that product categories like footwear and clothing contribute significantly to revenue, while customer demographics such as age and gender influence spending behavior.

It was observed that discounts do not always lead to higher purchase value, and repeat customers play a crucial role in maintaining consistent sales. Additionally, the preference for digital payment methods highlights the growing trend of online transactions.

Overall, this analysis provides valuable insights that can help businesses improve customer targeting, optimize product strategies, and make data-driven decisions to enhance overall performance.
